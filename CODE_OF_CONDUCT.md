# Code Of Conduct

## Our Pledge

Contributors are expected to keep project discussions respectful, constructive,
and focused on the work.

## Expected Behavior

- Be specific and technical when giving feedback.
- Assume mistakes can be fixed.
- Respect different levels of experience with ArchiMate, React, TypeScript, and
  browser APIs.
- Keep discussions on issues and pull requests relevant to the project.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Repeated off-topic disruption.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Deliberately submitting malicious code, models, scripts, or extensions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues; reject pull
requests; or block users who violate this code of conduct.
